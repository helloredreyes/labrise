mini-kitchen + cooking ware

For those who enjoy cooking, the condo is equipped with a compact yet fully functional kitchenette. Basic cooking equipment, including a stove, microwave, refrigerator, and essential utensils, allows you to prepare simple meals. Whether you're whipping up a quick breakfast or a cozy dinner, you’ll find everything you need to enjoy home-cooked meals away from home.

workout + swim

Staying fit while traveling is convenient with the property workout equipment provided. Running machines, dumbbells, and swimming pool are available for your exercise routine. For a more comprehensive workout, you can take advantage of the condo's access to a fully equipped gym within the building.
